  Who We Are

Dedicated to Protecting Girls' Health & Dignity

Adolescent Girls Initiative for Reproductive Health Liberty (A'GIRHL) is a youth-centered organization dedicated to protecting the health, dignity, and rights of adolescent girls.

We provide accurate, age-appropriate reproductive health education and connect girls to safe, stigma-free services so they can make informed choices about their bodies and futures.

Through community engagement, partnerships, and advocacy, we work to break the silence around reproductive health and create a world where every girl can live freely, without shame, and reach her full potential.

Age-Appropriate Education — Providing accurate, accessible reproductive health knowledge to adolescent girls.

Stigma-Free Services — Connecting girls to safe and confidential reproductive health services.

Community Advocacy — Breaking silence through campaigns, partnerships, and policy engagement.

Meet Our Executive Director

Thamar Bature

Executive Director — A'GIRHL

Executive Director — A'GIRHL

Thamar Bature

Championing Girls' Health & Rights

Thamar Bature is the Executive Director of A'GIRHL, leading the organization's vision to empower adolescent girls across communities. With a deep commitment to reproductive health rights, dignity, and youth empowerment, she drives programs that transform lives.

Through strategic partnerships, community engagement, and unwavering advocacy, she has built A'GIRHL into a trusted voice for adolescent girls — ensuring they have the knowledge, support, and confidence to make informed choices about their health and futures.

"Every girl deserves the knowledge, confidence, and support needed to make informed decisions about her health and future. Together, we are building a generation of empowered girls who can thrive without fear, stigma, or limitation."
